# Research Critique Prompt

Review the selected OneMind text as a skeptical research collaborator.

Focus on:

- unclear assumptions,
- overclaims or deployment-sounding language,
- missing definitions,
- weak solvability-boundary reasoning,
- privacy, consent, governance, misuse, or over-dependence risks,
- places where a claim should become a proposition, conjecture, hypothesis, or
  open question,
- missing related-work neighborhoods.

Use synthetic examples only. Do not introduce private, sensitive, identifying,
confidential, or non-consensual real-world details.

Return concise findings with file or section references, followed by suggested
edits or questions for the maintainer.
